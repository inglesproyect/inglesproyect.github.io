Attractions

Mompox is home to various attractions, such as the Mompox Cathedral, the Puente de los Espejos, and its scenic riverside, making it a perfect destination for tourists seeking historical and natural beauty.

Events

Mompox is famous for its annual events like the Festival of Religious Music and the Festivities of the Holy Cross, attracting visitors from all over the country and abroad.

Gastronomy

The gastronomy of Mompox is a reflection of its rich cultural heritage. Traditional dishes such as "mote de queso" and "carne en su jugo" are popular among locals and visitors alike.
